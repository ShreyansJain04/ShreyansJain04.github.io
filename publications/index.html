  • IEEE SAUS
    Suppressing Streak Artifacts Generated by the Interference of Imaging and Therapy Fields: Initial Findings Using a Hybrid U-Net and Diffusion Model
    Mihir Agarwal, Shreyans Jain, Ruchika Dhawan, and 2 more authors
    In IEEE South Asian Ultrasonics Symposium, Mar 2024
    Abs PDF

    We present a combined U-Net and diffusion model to remove streak artifacts in therapeutic ultrasound imaging. Using a denoising U-Net with skip connections and text-based prompts for enhanced inpainting, initial results show effective artifact removal, an improved Signal-to-Noise Ratio by 6.0 ± 3.4 dB, and an IOU score of 0.698. Rapid model convergence suggests potential for real-time clinical use. Future work will validate the model on diverse datasets and optimize performance.

  • ISTU
    Reconstruction of Ultrasound Super-Resolution Images using a Hybrid Attention-based U-Net Architecture applied to sparse data
    Ruchika Dhawan, Mihir Agarwal, Shreyans Jain, and 2 more authors
    In International Society for Therapeutic Ultrasound Conference, Apr 2024
    Abs

    We developed a Hybrid Attention-Based U-Net architecture to enhance Ultrasound Localization Microscopy by accurately tracking microbubbles in noisy image sequences. Our approach reduces data requirements by 40 times and achieves a peak SNR enhancement of 14.73 dB in simulated data.
